Meatloaf

Ingredients

  • 600g Ground Beef
  • 1 Egg
  • Spices
    • Italian
    • Paprika
    • Garlic Powder
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

  2. In a mixing bowl, mix ground beef with the egg, spices, and bread crumbs.

  3. Form the meat into a loaf and put into a baking pan.

  4. Bake for 24-28 minutes
