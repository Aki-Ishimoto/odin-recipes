Orange Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 cup orange marmalade
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup Kansas City-style BBQ sauce
  • 1 pound of chicken nuggets

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Place nuggets on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  2. Bake nuggets in preheated oven until hot and crispy, 11-13 minutes or according to package directions.
  3. In a saucepan, combine orange marmalade, soy sauce, and BBQ sauce. Heat over low heat until melted and combined.
  4. Once nuggets are done, remove from oven and toss with the sauce until evenly coated.
