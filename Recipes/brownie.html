Mug Brownie

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and salt.
  2. Add the water, canola oil, vanilla extract, and cinnamon. Stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave on high for 1 minute or until the brownie is cooked through.
  4. Let it cool for a minute before enjoying!
