Hamburger


Burgers are one of the most iconic food's in the America's, found at almost every corner and fast food business. Plus a great food to make with mostly little work, you're going to need:

  • 1KG Ground Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Sliced Cheese

This recipe makes 4 hamburgers, divide the ground beef into 4 sections, roll them up into a ball then you can squish the hamburger shape with your hands, make sure to flatten them out as much as possible as the meat contracts when cooking making them smaller than when you first rolled them. Doing this also helps with making them cook faster. Preheat your skillet to medium-high heat, put the patties on, depending on the size of the patties, they can cook for 5-8 minutes on each side. Watch out for smoke as that is an indicator that that side might be done earlier than normal. After the burgers are nearly done cooking, grab your plate and put buns on them, open them up, and put them in the microwave for 20 seconds, after that, slap a slice of cheese on those bad boys then slap the burger right on top. You got yourself a delicious burger waiting to be eaten by you.
